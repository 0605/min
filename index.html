Meet Min.

A smarter, faster web browser.

Find anything instantly

The searchbar answers your questions immediately, with information from DuckDuckGo including Wikipedia entries, a calculator, and more. Jump to any site quickly with fuzzy search, and get suggestions before you even start typing. And when you create a bookmark, the full text of the page is saved and becomes searchable, so you’ll never lose anything again.

Effortless tab management

Tabs in Min open to the right of the current tab, so you’ll never lose your place. Tabs you haven’t looked at in a while dim, so you can focus on what you’re working on. And when you have lots of tabs, you can swipe down to expand your tabs into a grid, allowing you to quickly find any tab.

Fast and efficient

Min is designed to be fast. It saves your battery, so you can work longer, without worrying about running out of power.

Open-Source software

Min is written entirely with CSS and JavaScript, and is open-source software, available on GitHub.

How to install Min

  • Drag Min from your downloads folder to the Applications folder.
  • If you see a message that "Min can’t be opened because it is from an unidentified developer":
    • Open System Preferences (from the Apple menu)
    • Click on "Security & Privacy"
    • Click "Open Anyway"
    • Enter your password if needed.
